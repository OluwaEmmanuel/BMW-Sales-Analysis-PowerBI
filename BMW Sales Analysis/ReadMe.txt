# 🚗 BMW Sales Data Analysis (2010–2024) — Power BI Project

## 📘 Overview
This project analyzes **BMW sales performance from 2010 to 2024** using Power BI.  
The goal was to uncover insights on sales trends, top-performing models, and regional performance patterns.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Visualize sales and revenue trends across the years  
- Identify top and bottom performing BMW models  
- Analyze regional contribution to total sales  
- Explore correlations between sales volume and revenue  
- Drill down into performance using interactive visuals  

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Microsoft Power BI**
- **CSV dataset** (BMW sales data)

## 📊 Visualizations Used
- Line Chart — for yearly sales and revenue trend  
- Bar Chart — for top/bottom performing models  
- Treemap — for regional sales distribution  
- Scatter Chart — for revenue vs. sales volume correlation  
- Decomposition Tree — for deep dive into sales drivers  

## 📈 Key Insights
- Sales showed consistent growth post-2015, with major spikes in 2018 and 2022.  
- The **X5** and **X3** models dominated overall revenue share.  
- **Lagos** and **Abuja** regions contributed the largest sales volume.  
- Clear correlation between sales volume and revenue across major regions.
